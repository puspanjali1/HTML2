Pseudo-Elements:-
-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------
::after - Insert content after the content of an element.
::before - Insert content before the content of an element.
::first-letter - Selects the first letter of the first line of a block element.
::first-line - Selects the first line of a block element.
::selection - Selects the portion of an element that is selected by a user.
Pseudo-Classes
:active - Applies while an element is being activated by a user.
:checked - Applies to a checked checkbox or radio button.
:default - Applies to the default button in a group of buttons.
:disabled - Applies to disabled elements.
:empty - Applies when an element has no children or text content.
:enabled - Applies to enabled elements.
:first-child - Applies to the first child of a parent element.
:first-of-type - Applies to the first occurrence of a specific type of element.
:focus - Applies while an element has focus.
:hover - Applies while an element is being hovered over by a pointer.
:in-range - Applies to elements with a value within a specified range.
:invalid - Applies to elements with an invalid value.
:lang() - Applies to elements with a specific lang attribute value.
:last-child - Applies to the last child of a parent element.
:last-of-type - Applies to the last occurrence of a specific type of element.
:link - Applies to unvisited links.
:not() - Applies to elements that do not match a specific selector.
:nth-child() - Applies to elements based on their position within a parent element.
:nth-last-child() - Applies to elements based on their position counting from the last child.
:nth-last-of-type() - Applies to elements based on their position counting from the last occurrence of a specific type of element.
:nth-of-type() - Applies to elements based on their position within a parent element, counting only elements of a specific type.
:only-of-type - Applies to elements that are the only ones of their type within a parent element.
:only-child - Applies to elements that are the only child of their parent element.
:optional - Applies to elements that are not required.
:out-of-range - Applies to elements with a value outside a specified range.
:read-only - Applies to elements that are read-only.
:read-write - Applies to elements that are read-write (not read-only).
:required - Applies to elements that are required.
:root - Applies to the root element of a document.
:target - Applies to the target element of a URL anchor link.
:valid - Applies to elements with a valid value.
:visited - Applies to visited links.
